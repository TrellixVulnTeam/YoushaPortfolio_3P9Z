Call of Duty: Black Ops 1

"Call of Duty: Black Ops 1" is my favourite game of all time. It was divided into 3 different game modes: Campaign, Multiplayer and Zombies. All 3 game modes were phenomenal.

Call of Duty: Black Ops 2

"Call of Duty: Black Ops 2" is one of my favourite games. It was also divided into 3 different game modes: Campaign, Multiplayer and Zombies. All 3 game modes were phenomenal as well.

Call of Duty: Black Ops 3

"Call of Duty: Black Ops 3" is one of my favourite games. It was also divided into 3 different game modes: Campaign, Multiplayer and Zombies. All 3 game modes were phenomenal as well, zombies being better than the prior games.

Minecraft

In Minecraft, players explore a blocky, procedurally-generated 3D world, and may discover and extract raw materials, craft tools, build structures or earthworks, and depending on game mode, can fight computer-controlled "mobs" and players.



Batman: Arkham City

The game is presented from the third-person perspective with a primary focus on Batman's combat and stealth abilities, detective skills, and gadgets that can be used in both combat and exploration. Batman can freely move around Arkham City.

Batman: Arkham Knight

The game is presented from a third-person perspective, with a primary focus on Batman's melee combat, stealth abilities, detective skills, and gadgets. Batman can freely move around the open world of Gotham City.

Fortnite

Fortnite is an online video game developed by Epic Games and released in 2017. It is available in three distinct game mode versions that otherwise share the same general gameplay and game engine.

Uncharted 2: Among Thieves

Uncharted 2 is an action-adventure platform video game played from a third-person view, with the player in control of Nathan Drake. Drake is physically adept and is able to jump, climb, and scale narrow ledges and wall-faces to get between points.



Uncharted 3: Drake's Deception

Uncharted 3: Drake's Deception is an action-adventure platform video game played from a third-person perspective, with the player in control of Nathan Drake.

Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege

Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege is a first-person shooter game, in which players utilize many different operators from the Rainbow team with different gadgets and abilities.

Destiny 2

Similar to its predecessor, Destiny 2 is a first-person shooter game that incorporates role-playing and massively multiplayer online game (MMO) elements.

Apex Legends

Apex Legends is an online multiplayer battle royale game featuring squads of three players using pre-made characters (called "Legends"), similar to those of hero shooters.
